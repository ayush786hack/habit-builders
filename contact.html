Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

What's the difference between Free and Premium plans?

The Free plan includes basic habit tracking, limited video content, and community access. The Premium plan adds AI assistant, unlimited coin earning, premium rewards, and advanced tracking features. Visit our Free vs Premium page for more details.

How does the reward system work?

You earn coins by completing your daily habits and submitting proof. These coins can be redeemed for rewards in our reward store. The more consistent you are, the more coins you'll earn!

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time. Your benefits will continue until the end of your billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your personal information and habit data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.
